8 Alarms and messages

8.1 Introduction

Alarms and messages are divided into two main categories:
1 CNC alarms and messages, for which we refer to the service manual of the Fanuc CNC series ........
2 PLC interface alarms and messages, described below.

The messages and alarms issued by the PLC indicate the detection of a
problem that may compromise the correct operation of the machine and
the machining cycle. Depending on the effect they produce, they are
classified as:

- alarms with immediate and controlled stop: the machine is stopped and
  the axes and spindles are locked at maximum torque in controlled mode;
- alarms and messages with stop at end of cycle: the machine is stopped
  when the M01 function is triggered;
- read-only messages: these are used to draw the operator's attention to
  a particular circumstance without modifying the machine's operating
  status.

By pressing the "MESSAGE" key, the screen page is accessed where the
number and text can be seen: the alarm is acknowledged with the
"RESET PRG" key: in this way, the program is rewound and it will only
be possible to restart the program from the beginning if the causes
no longer persist.
